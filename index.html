You are the most extraordinary person I have ever known. The way you carry your heart — openly, bravely, without armour — makes me want to be softer, braver, better. You make me want to be the kind of person who deserves you. Every single day.

My love letter to you

My love,

I don't know how to write a love letter. Every time I try, the words feel too small, too flat, too ordinary for what I'm trying to say. But here goes:

You are the thing that happened to me that I didn't plan for. I had a version of my life mapped out — and then you walked into it, and nothing has been the same since. Not in a chaotic way. In the way that you look at a painting you've passed a hundred times and suddenly, one day, you actually see it — and you don't know how you ever missed it.

That is you. I don't know how I ever missed you. I don't know how I thought I was okay before you. I wasn't missing anything — until I found you. And now I know what it feels like to have something real, and I never want to not have you.
— from somewhere deep

You are the first thought in the morning and the last one at night. You are the person I want to tell everything to — the good things, the stupid things, the things that don't make sense yet. You are home. And today I asked if I could be yours — forever.

Yours, completely and without reservation — always. 💍
