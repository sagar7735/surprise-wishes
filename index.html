What you mean to me

You have changed me. Not dramatically — not in ways the world would notice. But quietly, deeply, in the places that matter most. You have made me gentler. More patient. More certain of what I want from this life. And what I want is you.
— from me, to you

Thank you for existing. Thank you for the way you exist — fully, beautifully, without apology. Thank you for letting me be part of your world.

My love letter to you

My love,

I don't know how to write a love letter. Every time I try, the words feel too small, too flat, too ordinary for what I'm trying to say. But here goes:

You are the thing that happened to me that I didn't plan for. I had a version of my life mapped out — and then you walked into it, and nothing has been the same since. Not in a chaotic way. In the way that you look at something you've passed a hundred times and suddenly, one day, you actually see it — and you don't know how you ever missed it.

That is you. I don't know how I ever missed you. I don't know how I thought I was okay before you. Now I know what it feels like to have something real, and I never want to not have you.
— from somewhere deep

You are the first thought in the morning and the last one at night. You are the person I want to tell everything to. You are home. And today I asked if I could be yours — forever.

Yours, completely and without reservation — always. 💍
